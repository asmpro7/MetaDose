# MetaDose 1.0.0

* Initial public release of the MetaDose package.

* Provides functions for linear and nonlinear dose-response meta-regression of study-level data for both continuous (mdcont) and binary (mdbin) outcomes.

* Includes built-in visualization of dose-response curves and associated print() and plot() S3 methods for easy inspection of model summaries and plots.
